PickleballFlow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

PickleballFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information Storage

Local Storage Only: PickleballFlow operates entirely offline. All data you create in the app is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Player names and lists
  • Game history and session data
  • Court assignments and scores
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device. We do not upload player names, game details, or any session information to our servers or third-party services.

Analytics Data Collection

PickleballFlow uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information that helps us improve the app. This service collects:

What We Collect:

  • Usage patterns: Which features you use (e.g., starting games, viewing history, managing player lists)
  • Session metrics: Anonymous statistics like number of courts, number of players, game duration, and games per session
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Screen navigation: Which screens you view within the app

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Player names or any personally identifiable information
  • Game scores or specific game results
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • User IDs or account information

All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated. Google handles this data under its own privacy policies. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Responsibilities

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for:

  • Managing and backing up your data
  • Exporting session history when needed
  • Deleting data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Third-Party Services

The app currently uses only Google Firebase Analytics as described above. We do not integrate advertising services, social media platforms, or other third-party data collectors.

Children's Privacy

PickleballFlow does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. All data remains local to the device, and no personal information is transmitted.

Future Changes

We may add features in the future that involve additional data collection or third-party services (such as cloud backup or social features). If we do:

  • These features will be optional
  • We will update this privacy policy
  • We will notify you within the app
  • You can choose whether to use these features

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: pickleball.flow@gmail.com

Summary

Bottom line: Your player names and game data stay on your device. We only collect anonymous usage statistics through Google Firebase Analytics to help us make the app better. No personal information is collected or shared.
